In the first lesson of Web102, we learnd about flex-container, justify-content, align-content and align-items.
The flex-container is a alternative of the normal ones which makes every child of the container a flex item.
The main-direction of the flex-container is controlled with justify-content, while the align-content controlls the
cross-direction. The align-items is for the controll over the items of the flex-container.

Speed-Coding fingertrick of the week

#container>div{$}*6
