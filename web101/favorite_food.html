Soup

A seefood congee is the rice soup consisting of schrimp,rice,green
onion and salt. It is unbelievably delicous and easy to cook.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Schrimp
  • Green onion
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Cook a reguler rice soup
  2. when the rice soup is cooked, add schrimp to the soup
  3. Cook the soup until the schrimp is cooked, too
  4. Add proper amount of salt
  5. Add green onion, stir a few times
  6. The delicious seefood congee is ready to serve
